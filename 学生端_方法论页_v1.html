CHAPTER 01

为什么 Part 2 总是卡壳

不是你准备不够——是方向不对。死记硬背 100 道题的方法天然失败。

01

CHAPTER 03 · 收益分析

每多掌握一个故事，覆盖题目数量大幅跳升——前 4 个故事覆盖 91% 题目。剩余 9% 考前一周再过即可。

CHAPTER 05 · 回答框架

四段框架 · 每段都有专属 Marker

把 2 分钟独白拆成 4 段，每段用一个 discourse marker 开场——考官 1 秒就知道你在哪个阶段。

S1
≈ 10 秒
Answer Directly
一句话直接回答题目，让考官立刻知道你在讲什么。
"I'm going to talk about..."
S2
≈ 20 秒
Background
从时间开始铺背景，给故事舞台，建立清晰背景。
"I remember, that was around..."
S3
≈ 70 秒
Details ⭐
主战场。情节、细节、5 拓展技巧全部在这里叠加使用。
"Now let me tell you..."
S4
≈ 20 秒
Prospect
朴实真实的收尾，未来打算或感受，不需要刻意升华。
"Anyway, [总结一句]..."
⚡
S3 是主战场 — 80% 的技巧、80% 的分数都在这里产生。接下来的 5 个拓展技巧，全部在 S3 叠加使用。

SKILL 01

主体 + 客体

描述事物（客体）时，立刻延伸到"我 / 感受 / 关联"（主体）。一个维度变两个，篇幅翻倍。

公式：客体（事物本身） → 主体（"我"或"我的感受/关联"）
🖥️ 描述物品 · MacBook
客体："Its battery life is super long..." → 主体："I'm a student, I don't need to carry a charger every time I leave my apartment — super convenient."
🌿 描述地点 · Park
客体："It's a small park near my apartment..." → 主体："For me, it's the most peaceful place in the city — I go there every weekend to clear my head."
🎬 描述电影 · Forrest Gump
客体："It tells the story of a man with a low IQ who lives an extraordinary life..." → 主体："It really inspired me — every time I want to give up, I think of Forrest just running."
SKILL 02

对比手法

任何主题都能找到对照组——瞬间多说 30 秒，同时突出主体的独特性。

公式：找一个对照组 → "好/坏"或"普通/特别"或"以前/现在"

SKILL 03

举例技巧

P1 叫"背景举例"，P3 叫"细化举例"——本质都是：用一个具体的时间 + 地点 + 场景，把抽象的一句话变成真实的画面。

公式：抽象观点 → "Just [时间], I [地点] [具体发生了什么]"
P1 · 背景举例
Q: Do you like walking?
"Absolutely, I love walking quite a lot. Actually, just yesterday morning, I went to a park near my home and walked around for about half an hour. The air was really fresh and it helped me clear my head before starting the day."
P3 · 细化举例
Q: Do you think young people today are less patient?
"I think so. For example, my friend Lucy told me she used to practice volleyball for three hours every single day — even when she was exhausted. That kind of patience is really rare now."
SKILL 04 · 新增

睹物思人

描述物品 / 地点 / 场景题时，从客体延伸到"另一个人物"——让答案瞬间有人物温度，避免冷冰冰的说明文。

公式：物 → 人 · "Whenever I see / use ___, I'm reminded of ___"
与 SKILL 01 区别：主体客体是"物 → 我自己"；睹物思人是"物 → 另一个人"。当题目要求里就有"我"（如 "describe a habit you want to develop"），先用 01；当题目偏物本身（如 "describe an important item"），用 04 引入人物。
📖 Q3 · Family recipe book → 爷爷
"It's an old handwritten recipe book... Whenever I open it, I'm reminded of my grandpa — he used to flip through these pages every morning, scribbling new ideas in the margins. The book itself isn't expensive, but every page carries his handwriting and his memory."
🎸 Q · Guitar → Lucy
"It's a small acoustic guitar... Whenever I see it sitting in the corner, I'm reminded of Lucy — she used to practice every morning at 6, singing along until her voice got hoarse. The guitar isn't really mine, but it makes me think of her every time."
🌳 Q · Childhood Park → 童年朋友
"It's a quiet little park behind my old elementary school... Whenever I walk past it, I'm reminded of my best friend from back then — we used to climb the trees together, race each other to the swings. The park is exactly the same, but everything else has changed."
SKILL 05

3D 成功公式 · 自律 + 好方法 + 天赋

聊到「某人为什么这么优秀 / 这么成功」——三个维度叠起来讲，内容立刻饱满，分数直接上台阶。

Why are they so good? → Self-discipline + Smart approach + Natural talent
🔥
D1 · 自律
Self-discipline
每天坚持 · 不偷懒
"gets up at 6am every morning"
🧠
D2 · 好方法
Smart approach
有规划 · 不蛮干
"follows a clear plan / has a system"
⭐
D3 · 天赋
Natural talent
天生敏感 · 底层优势
"has a natural feel for it"
🎯 完整示范 · Lucy 为什么是国家队排球员
[D1 自律] She gets up at 6am every single morning to train — no excuses, not even on weekends.
[D2 方法] And it's not random practice — she follows a strict training plan designed with her coach, plus a low-calorie low-fat diet to stay in shape.
[D3 天赋] On top of all that, she has a natural feel for the game — her reactions are sharper than most, and she reads the court instinctively.
→ 三句话下来，整个 TP 有了：勤奋 + 聪明 + 天赋，缺一不可。
💡 D1 自律 · 万金油用法

凡是聊到「技能 / 学习 / 改变 / 习惯」——都可以单独套 self-discipline 万能句，即便不三维度一起讲也是加分项。

CHAPTER 07 · 实战

S3 里，技巧是可以叠加的

高分不是来自"用了一个技巧"——而是在 S3 里把多个技巧自然地叠在一起。

🍳 COOKING · Q1 · S3 完整示范
Describe a time you needed to use your imagination
S3 MARKER
Now let me tell you how I used my imagination.
展开 1SKILL 01 · 主体客体
Our teacher said: no fast food — too boring. So I had to think of something creative. I came up with Fruit Roast Chicken20 — fruits mixed with chicken. The dish itself looked mouth-watering. And for me personally, it was the first time I realized I actually had some talent for cooking.
展开 2SKILL 02 · 对比
Compare this to what I would've done before — I would've just ordered KFC like everyone else. Boring, predictable, no story to tell. But this time, I pushed myself out of my comfort zone, and the result was completely different.
展开 3SKILL 03 · 举例细化
When I brought it to the potluck, everyone went silent for a second — then they all reached out to try a piece. Lucy said it tasted "weird but amazing". That moment was when I knew the imagination paid off.
🎯
看完这段你应该感受到：S3 里的 70 秒不是匀速描述——是一波一波叠加的小高潮。每个技巧都为下一个技巧让出空间，最后落到 S4 的"From then on, I'm always looking for new ideas in the kitchen."
